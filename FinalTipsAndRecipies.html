Tips

  • Track your food: Use and app or join a program (Weightwatchers is great! I speak from experience.)
  • Stay Hydrated!
  • Try to exercise regularly. Start small, do 30 minutes to begin. Just keep going consistently.
  • Try to ditch sugar and sweets.
  • Cut back on portion sizes.
  • Icorporate more fruit and veggies in your diet.
  • Don't deprive yourself of foods you like, just eat enough to satisy cravings.
  • Make a list when you grocery shop, it keeps down on spontanious purchases.
  • Cook at home more than ordering takeout.
  • Have a support network. Your in this for the long haul!
